Nebuleuse is an Open Source solution for video game developpers to integrate online account for their players to their games with minimal effort.
Easily integrate statistics, achievements and more to your game.

Users

Recognize your players and get their informations ingame

Achievements

Have achievements for your players to unlock. Automatically stored and updated directly in your game

Statistics

Track progress of your players and store meaningful information about their game sessions. Store kills, points, levels and others
