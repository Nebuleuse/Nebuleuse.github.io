Nebuleuse is a self hosted Open Source solution for video game developers to integrate online account for their players to their games with minimal effort.
Easily integrate statistics, achievements and more to your game.

Why use it ?

Self-Hosted

You host the server yourself, this means everyone can deploy and use such features but also that only you has access to it

Achievements, stats and more

Have achievements for your players to unlock, stats about the player behavior, and more directly into your game

Administration dashboard

The fully featured administration panel allows you to edit achievements, stats and such, as well as presenting you meaningful data about what's happening
